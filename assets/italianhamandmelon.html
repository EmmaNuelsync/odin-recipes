Italian Ham and Melon

Italian Ham and Melon are paired to make a super easy, 2-ingredient, refreshing appetizer or snack: just cut the cantaloupe into bite-sized pieces, wrap them with ham and spear them on skewers! Use superior-quality Italian ham for best results



Prep Time:

10 mins

Total Time:

10 mins

Servings:

4


Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe seeded and cut into 8 wedges

  • 8 thin slices prosciutto


Directions


Step 1

Remove the flesh from the rind of the cantaloupe; wrap each piece of cantaloupe with a slice of prosciutto. Arrange on a platter and serve cold
