Air Fryer Eggplant Parmesan

These air fryer eggplant Parmesan rounds are oil-free, yet they are crispy and crunchy. All thanks to an air fryer! They are good eaten right out of the air fryer or topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese. Either way, they are delicious.



Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

20 mins

Total Time:

35 mins

Servings:

4


Ingredients

  • Half cup of italian bread crumbs

  • Quarter cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese

  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • Half teaspoon dried basil

  • Half teaspoon garlic powder

  • Half teaspoon onion powder

  • Half teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

  • Quarter cup flour

  • 2 large eggs, beaten

  • 1 medium eggplant, skiced into 1/2 inch rounds

  • 1 cup marinara sauce, or more to taste

  • 8 slices mozzarella cheese, or as needed


Directions


Step 1

Combine bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, salt, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper in a shallow bowl. Place flour in a seperatte shallow bowl and beaten eggs in a third shallow bowl.



Step 2

Dip sliced eggplant first in flour, then in beaten eggs, and finally coat with bread crumb mixture. Place coated eggplant on tray and let rest for 5 minutes



Step 3

Preheat an air fryer to 370 degrees F (185 degrees C)



Step 4

Place breaded eggplant rounds in the air fryer basket, making sue they are not touching; work in batches if necessary. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes, flip each round, and cook until desired crispiness is achieved, 4 to 6 minutes more.



Step 5

Top each eggplant round with marinara sauce and 1 slice of mozzarella cheese. Place the basket back in the air fryer and cook until cheese has started to melt, 1 to 2 minutes. Repeat with remaining eggplant, if necessary.



Step 6

Serve and enjoy
