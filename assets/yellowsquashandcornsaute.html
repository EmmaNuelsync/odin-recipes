Yellow Squash and Corn Saute

Use summer squash and leftover corn on the cob to make this pretty side dish that's super tasty



Prep Time:

10 mins

Cook Time:

25 mins

Total Time:

35 mins

Servings:

4


Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, husked and cleaned

  • 2 yellow squash, diced

  • half cup water

  • 2 tablespoons butter, or more to taste

  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parley (Optional)

  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste


Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients



Step 2

Place corn into a large pot and cover with sslted water; bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain and set aside to cool.



Step 3

While the corn is cooling, place squash into a skillet and add water. Cook over medium heat until tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess water from the skillet.



Step 4

Cut kernels off the cobs. Stir corn, butter, parsley, salt and pepper into squash; cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through, about 5 minutes
